Customer Service
Our customer service provides tech support for all of our products. We can use our state-of-the-art equipment to diagnose and trouble shoot problems you may have with our equipment in the future.
Our "Geniuses" are available on our tech support line (which is located on the website) or at any one of our retail stores.
We have a 60-day full-refund return policy upon purchase to return any defective products that may have missed our rigorous quality control team. After the 60-day period you have another 30-day return policy for credit or replacement for a defective product.

Product Return
If you bought it at our retail store you can return it there. If you bought it at our online store all you have to do is:
1. Fill out a return form stating why you are returning the product. This is so we have a record on form that you wish to return the product.
2. Once you have sent the form, we will send a box for return. Just put the defective product in the box and send it in the mail. Shipping is already paid.
3. Once we receive the product you will receive an email updating on the status of your product whether it will be refurbished or if you will receive a new product or a refund.
There will no refunds for digital purchases.
