It all started with a dream ...

Apple Inc. was founded on April 1, 1976 by recent college dropouts Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak. Their goal was to change the way people view computers, and wanted to have them small enough to fit in offices and homes.

Growing..

Apple continued to grow throughout the 80s with revolutionary color graphics on computers and had the highest profits in 1990. In 1997, Apple implemented a Mac version of computer software. Up until present day Apple Inc. is creating computers, laptops, and phones with revolutionary technology.
